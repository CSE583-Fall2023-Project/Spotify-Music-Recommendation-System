It should be useful to look at the distribution to gain a better sense of the variation of this variable.

The shape of the distribution appears to be roughly unimodal with a peak around the 60%-70% range, and the 70%-80% range also has a high frequency, though slightly less than the previous bin. The fertility rate ranging from approximately 30%-40% has the lowest frequency, close to 0.
